<p><b>A Digital Budget Tracker for Managing Student Allowances<b></p>
<br>
<p>The purpose of this project is for the development of a digital budget tracker. It is a design to help students manage their allowances effectively. The system will allow users to record their daily expenses, categorize spending and tracking the remaining funds. By providing a simple tool to help organize and handle the finances, this budget tracker encourages a responsible money management and reduces overspending for us as a student. This also calculates how much you spent and how many allowances are remaining.</p>
<br>
<b><h1>Project Objectives</h1></b>
<hr>
<ul>
  <li>To provide the simple tool for the students to track their daily expenses.</li>
  <li>To help students understand and manage their spending habits.</li>
  <li>To promote some financial literacy and responsible budgeting for students.</li>
  <li>To prevent overspending by providing a clear record of allowance usage.</li>
  <li>To encourage the students and help them to save their savings and improve the long-term money management skills.</li>
</ul>
